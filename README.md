Elevator Pitch

A full-stack web platform designed to help teachers, students, and prayer participants communicate faster and more effectively in online Bible study and daily prayer programs. It streamlines group coordination, resource sharing, and real-time interaction, making virtual ministry more organized and accessible.

Problem Statement

Online Bible study programs often involve around 100 students, requiring leaders to divide participants into multiple small groups and coordinate facilitators. Currently, communication relies heavily on email, which is slow, inefficient, and difficult to manage, leading to delays and organizational challenges.

Target User

A church ministry leader responsible for organizing online Bible study classes and daily prayer sessions via Zoom, who needs an efficient way to manage participants, assign groups, share materials, and communicate quickly with both facilitators and students.

Why This Project

This project is personally meaningful because it was inspired by a real need from a deacon leading online Bible study programs. It allows me to apply my technical skills to support ministry work—helping spread the Gospel through accessible online learning and making it easier for people everywhere to participate in Bible study and prayer.
